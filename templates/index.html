Our Mission

With our extensive experience as traders spanning over 5 years, we have dedicated countless hours to honing our skills through manual strategy development, rigorous backtesting, emotional management, and tackling the myriad of challenges that traders encounter. Recognizing the need for a computer-based solution, we embarked on a quest to overcome these obstacles. After investing thousands of additional hours into crafting and formulating an intricate feedforward neural-network, our goal has always remained to deliver this technology to retail traders everywhere, despite similar software only being found in the hands of hedge funds and investment firms. While we acknowledge that our solution is not the end all be all, by removing much of the human error which occurs while trading, we aspire to assist you on your journey towards becoming a more consistent and profitable trader. Let our indicators and algorithms serve as guiding lights, leading you towards the right steps.
